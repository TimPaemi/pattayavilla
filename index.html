★ WHAT IS PATTAYA VILLA STREAM

A villa. A camera.
Every single night.

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is the nightly livestream by Tim & Paemi — a German-Thai married couple broadcasting from their villa in Pattaya, Thailand. Every night at 9 PM ICT, the cameras turn on and don't turn off for six to eight hours. Then they do it again the next night. No exceptions, no nights off.

The show is unscripted but not chaotic. The music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts. The villa is the entire stage — Tim, Paemi, and a rotating group of close friends stay inside for the whole show. There are no outside shoots, no calls to strangers, no chasing viral moments. What happens is what happens — challenges, eating, playing games, talking, laughing, and constant back-and-forth with the chat.

Chat is the room. That's the whole format. Tim and Paemi respond to whatever lands — questions, jokes, dares, topics. If a Super Chat hits, it gets read on air and the donor gets a live shoutout. No names ever get published on this website — the recognition stays in the moment, on the stream.

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is operated by Pattaya-Authority.com (TIMPAEMI Co., Ltd.), the parent company behind a 14-site Pattaya network covering restaurants, gyms, visa help, coffee, schools, medical, pets, vehicle rentals, personal training, Greek souvlaki, and outdoor content. Total network reach is 5,000,000+ followers across 60+ social accounts, with the villa livestream as the flagship nightly broadcast since 2024.

The show has no sponsors and takes no brand deals — it's funded by viewers through YouTube. Support stays simple: subscribe, watch, share, Super Chat, Super Thanks. Nothing off-platform. The full story is on the about page; every path is on the support page.

★ NEW HERE

Your first night.

Never watched before? Every night stands on its own. There's no episode one, no lore, no inside story you missed. Open the stream at 9 PM Bangkok time — or whenever you can after that; it runs six to eight hours, so there's no such thing as late. It's free on @timpaemi live or via timpaemi.com/live.

Say hi in chat when you arrive — new viewers asking obvious questions are genuinely welcome, and the regulars usually answer before we do. The show is mainly in English; you're welcome to say hi in any language. Two minutes on the chat code of conduct tells you how the room works. The format page has the full walkthrough.

Want your message read on air? A Super Chat pins it to the top and earns a live shoutout. Just want to watch? That's the whole point — watch time is the best free support there is. Still have questions? The FAQ covers the rest.

What time for you?

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM goes live every night at 9:00 PM Bangkok time — ICT, UTC+7. Thailand doesn't use daylight saving, so the Pattaya start time never moves. Here's when the villa stream begins where you are.
