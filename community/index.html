// 03 — THE ROOM

Anonymous · family · open door

Why no names.

Every other channel turns supporters into a leaderboard. Donor walls. Top contributor of the month. Animated alerts that show the amount and the username every time someone tips.

We don't do that. The villa show stays a room, not a stage. If you support us, that's between you and us — not you and an audience.

This page exists to honor the people who keep the lights on. It doesn't exist to rank them or expose them.

  • No published donation amounts.
  • No supporter rankings or leaderboards.
  • No public list of mod, supporter, or regular handles.
  • No "top fan" badges, animated tip alerts with usernames, or donor walls.
  • Tim & Paemi are the only people we name on this site.
  • Every supporter — free, paid, monthly, one-time — counts the same.
