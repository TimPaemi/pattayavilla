// 03 — THE ROOM

THE REGULARS.

Chat names you'll see most nights. Some have been around since the first stream. They keep the room warm, they answer new viewers' questions, they remember the running jokes.

If you stick around long enough, you become one of them. There's no signup. There's no badge.

Anonymous · family · open door
★ HOW TO BECOME A REGULAR

Three steps. No badge required.

  1. Show up. Watch live, every night you can. Even 20 minutes counts. The algorithm sees you, and so does the chat.
  2. Be in chat. Say hi when you arrive. Reply to other viewers. Drop the kind of comments you'd want to read yourself. The regulars are just people who kept showing up and being human.
  3. Don't try. There's no “regular” application. No badge to earn. After enough nights, the room knows you. That's the only step that matters.
