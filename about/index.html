// 01 — THE STORY

How this started.

Tim is from Germany. Paemi is from Thailand. We met, fell in love, got married, and built a life in a villa in Pattaya — the kind of life you don't plan and can't really replicate.

The cameras came later. We were already having friends over most nights — eating, arguing about pointless things, taking dares, cooking weird food Paemi swears is delicious. Someone said we should put a camera up. So we did. People watched. They came back. They brought friends. They told us what they wanted to see. We listened, and the chat became the room.

That was 2024. We haven't taken a night off since.

// 02 — THE SHOW

What this is.

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is a live, unscripted, six-to-eight-hour broadcast from our villa, every single night. 9 PM Bangkok time, with no nights off and no exceptions — sick nights, holidays, our birthdays, we're still live.

The music is copyright-free and queued before we go live. The villa is the entire set — we never leave it during a stream. The crew is a small group of close friends who rotate through most nights. The conversation is whatever chat lands on and we run with. The format page walks through every part of it; the FAQ covers everything else people ask.

// 04 — THE PRINCIPLES

What we lock down.

A short list of things we never change. None of these are marketing — they're the reason the show will still work five years from now.

  • No sponsors, ever. A sponsor wants a deliverable. Chat wants a conversation. You can only serve one.
  • YouTube-only support. Subscribe, Super Chat, Super Thanks — and that's the entire list. No PayPal, no Patreon, nothing off-platform. The support page spells it out.
  • Live shoutouts on stream, anonymous on site. Every donor gets read on air, name and all. Nobody's name ever goes on this website — no leaderboards, no donor walls, no “top fan” badges.
  • Tim & Paemi are the only people named here. The friends in the villa, the mods, the regulars in chat — they stay anonymous on the website and get loved on stream. See the community page for the full policy.
  • We never leave the villa during a stream. No outside footage, no club tours, no random calls. The villa is the show.
  • The chat code of conduct isn't decoration — read the house rules before you arrive in chat for the first time and you'll fit right in.
