FAQ.

Common questions about the villa show. When, where, how, who. Tap a question to expand.

UPDATED REGULARLY · ASK NEW QUESTIONS IN CHAT
When do you stream?

Every night at 9 PM Bangkok time (ICT). 6 to 8 hours per stream. No nights off.

Where do I watch?

YouTube — @timpaemi live channel. The short link timpaemi.com/live always points to the current broadcast.

Is it free to watch the stream?

Yes — completely free. PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is a public live broadcast on YouTube. There is no paywall, no subscription fee, and no membership. Super Chat and Super Thanks are optional ways to support the show — but they are never required to watch.

Who are Tim and Paemi?

Tim and Paemi are a married couple who host the stream from their villa in Pattaya — Tim is from Germany, Paemi is from Thailand. They are the only two people named publicly on this website. The friends, mods, and regulars who appear on stream stay anonymous on the site, by design. The about page has the longer story.

What happens on the stream?

Six to eight hours of villa life — challenges, games, food, conversation, and constant back-and-forth with the chat. It is unscripted but not random: the music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts, and Tim, Paemi, and a rotating group of friends stay inside the villa the whole night. The format page has the full breakdown.

What time is the stream where I live?

The stream starts at 9 PM Bangkok time — ICT, UTC+7 — every night. Thailand does not use daylight saving, so the Pattaya start time never changes. The homepage timezone guide shows the start time for major cities around the world.

Can I watch the stream later if I miss it live?

The live broadcast is the main event, but uploaded recordings stay on the YouTube channel after each stream ends — that is also where Super Thanks works. For the real experience, watch live: the chat is the room, and the room only exists in real time.

What language is the stream in?

Mainly English. Tim speaks English and German, Paemi speaks Thai and English, and English is the default in chat — but you are welcome to say hi in any language. The show is easy to follow wherever you are from.

Do you stream every day, including weekends and holidays?

Yes — every single night, weekends and holidays included. The stream has run nightly since 2024 with no nights off. It always starts at 9 PM ICT and runs six to eight hours, wrapping up around 2 to 3 AM Bangkok time.

Do you have sponsors?

No. PATTAYA VILLA STREAM has no sponsors and takes no brand deals. A sponsor wants a deliverable; the chat wants a conversation — and you can only serve one. The show is funded by viewers through YouTube. The support page explains how that works.

Do you take requests?

We don't take music requests — the music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts. But we read every Super Chat, and chat questions drive the conversation. Drop a Super Chat to surface your question to the top.

Can I become a moderator?

Mods are invited, never applied for. Be active in chat for 30+ nights, respectful, and known to the regulars. We approach you — don't DM about it.

How do I support without spending money?

Subscribe with the bell on YouTube, watch live each night, and share the stream link with friends. All free, all matter. See the free support paths for every option.

How do I support with money?

Right now: Super Chat or Super Thanks during the stream. Both run through YouTube — Super Chat pins your message live in chat, Super Thanks works on uploaded VODs after the stream ends. Every donation gets a live shoutout on stream. More paths will open as we grow.

Do you talk politics or religion?

No. Both are banned in chat too. We keep the room a room — no debates, no fights, no flag-planting.

Do you ever stream from other places?

No. We never stream from anywhere except the villa. That's the whole format — the villa is the show. No outside footage, no club tours, no travel streams.

How do I contact you?

Email info@pattayastream.com, or message on LINE (@timpaemi) or WhatsApp (+66 96 728 6999).

Question not answered? Ask in chat.

The best place to ask anything is during the live stream. Drop a Super Chat and it goes to the top of the queue.
