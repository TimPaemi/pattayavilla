// SCHEDULE & WATCH

What is PATTAYA VILLA STREAM?

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is the nightly Pattaya villa livestream on YouTube hosted by Tim and Paemi — a German-Thai married couple broadcasting from their villa in Pattaya, Thailand. Six to eight hours every night at 9 PM ICT: challenges, games, food, conversation, and chat driving the show. Free to watch. Full story on the about page; watch tonight on the watch-live page.

What YouTube channel is the Pattaya livestream on?

The official channel is YouTube @timpaemi. The live broadcast URL is youtube.com/@timpaemi/live. The short link timpaemi.com/live always points to the current stream. Step-by-step guide on the watch page.

Is there a Pattaya livestream every night?

Yes — every single night since 2024, no nights off. The Pattaya villa show starts at 9 PM Bangkok time (ICT) and runs six to eight hours. Weekends and holidays included. See the typical night breakdown or the watch schedule section.

When do you stream?

Every night at 9 PM Bangkok time (ICT). 6 to 8 hours per stream. No nights off. Full schedule breakdown on the format page. Add the nightly show to your calendar (.ics, 9 PM ICT daily).

Where do I watch?

YouTube — @timpaemi live channel. The short link timpaemi.com/live always points to the current broadcast. Full watch guide on the watch-live page. Never watched before? The new-here guide on the about page has the short version.

Is it free to watch the stream?

Yes — completely free. PATTAYA VILLA STREAM is a public live broadcast on YouTube. There is no paywall, no subscription fee, and no membership. Optional paid support via Super Chat and Super Thanks — or see the free support paths. Neither is required to watch.

What time is the stream where I live?

The stream starts at 9 PM Bangkok time — ICT, UTC+7 — every night. Thailand does not use daylight saving, so the Pattaya start time never changes. The homepage timezone guide shows the start time for major cities around the world. Watching from Thailand long-term? Our visa help site covers the basics. Download the calendar file for a recurring 9 PM ICT reminder.

Can I watch the stream later if I miss it live?

The live broadcast is the main event, but uploaded recordings stay on the YouTube channel after each stream ends — Super Thanks works on those uploads. For the real experience, watch live: the chat is the room, and the room only exists in real time. See live vs recordings on the format page.

What language is the stream in?

Mainly English. Tim speaks English and German, Paemi speaks Thai and English, and English is the default in chat — but you are welcome to say hi in any language. The show is easy to follow wherever you are from. More on the format page.

Do you stream every day, including weekends and holidays?

Yes — every single night, weekends and holidays included. The stream has run nightly since 2024 with no nights off. It always starts at 9 PM ICT and runs six to eight hours, wrapping up around 2 to 3 AM Bangkok time. See the format page for how each night is structured.

// THE SHOW

Who are Tim and Paemi?

Tim and Paemi are a married couple who host the stream from their villa in Pattaya — Tim is from Germany, Paemi is from Thailand. They are the only two people named publicly on this website. The friends, mods, and regulars who appear on stream stay anonymous on the site, by design. The about page has the longer story; the TIMPAEMI brand site covers press and the wider network.

What happens on the stream?

Six to eight hours of villa life — challenges, games, food, conversation, and constant back-and-forth with the chat. It is unscripted but not random: the music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts, and Tim, Paemi, and a rotating group of friends stay inside the villa the whole night. Chat is the room — see the format breakdown for how that works.

Do you have sponsors?

No. PATTAYA VILLA STREAM has no sponsors and takes no brand deals. A sponsor wants a deliverable; the chat wants a conversation — and you can only serve one. The show is funded by viewers through YouTube — see free and paid support paths.

Do you take requests?

We don't take music requests — the music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts (see locked format rules). But we read every Super Chat, and chat questions drive the conversation. Drop one to surface your question to the top.

Do you talk politics or religion?

No. Both are banned in chat too — see the code of conduct. We keep the room a room — no debates, no fights, no flag-planting.

Do you ever stream from other places?

No. We never stream from anywhere except the villa. That's the whole format — the villa is the show. No outside footage, no club tours, no travel streams. Locked on the format page.

// SUPPORT & COMMUNITY

Can I become a moderator?

Mods are invited, never applied for. Be active in chat for 30+ nights, respectful, and known to the regulars. We approach you — don't DM about it. Expectations are in the chat code and on the community mod-invite section.

How do I support without spending money?

Subscribe with the bell on YouTube, watch live each night, and share the stream link with friends. All free, all matter. See the free support paths for every option.

How do I support with money?

Right now: Super Chat or Super Thanks through YouTube. Super Chat pins your message live in chat during the broadcast. Super Thanks works on uploaded VODs after the stream ends. Every donation gets a live shoutout on stream. More paths will open as we grow.

What's the difference between Super Chat and Super Thanks?

Super Chat pins your message live during the broadcast — use it when you want a shoutout or an answer on air. Super Thanks supports a specific uploaded recording after the stream ends. Both run through YouTube. See Super Chat vs Super Thanks plus the Super Chat and Super Thanks step guides on the support page.

Why don't you name mods or regulars on this website?

Mods and regulars get recognition on stream, not on the site — that's deliberate. Only Tim and Paemi are named publicly here. Everyone else stays anonymous online so the chat stays about the show, not status. More on the community recognition policy.

What gets you banned from chat?

Politics, religion, hate speech, spam, begging, and repeated rule-breaking. One warning on the first offense for most stuff — then a ban. The full list lives on the instant-ban section of the code of conduct.
