// HOUSE RULES · NON-NEGOTIABLE

CHAT
CODE.

Short and blunt. This is a chat-driven live show, not a debate club. Volunteer mods run the room. Be cool — we never have to say a word.

ENFORCED NIGHTLY · ONE WARNING · THEN PERMANENT BAN
JUMP TO: 01 · Why rules exist 02 · Welcome 03 · Instant ban 04 · Warnings 05 · Chat is the room 06 · Mods 07 · First night

Why the villa has a code.

PATTAYA VILLA STREAM runs live for 6 to 8 hours every night, hosted by Tim & Paemi (timpaemi.com), and the chat is the room — it's where the whole show happens. Operated by Pattaya-Authority.com — see the 14-site Pattaya network on the about page. A room that size only stays fun if everyone knows the line. This page is that line, in plain English. Back the show via the free support paths or read the community policy first.

The chat is kept in shape by a small team of volunteer moderators — unpaid regulars who give up their evenings so nobody else has to think about it. They're not security guards; they're the reason a stream this long never turns into a fight. Respect them and you'll never notice they're there.

Nothing below is complicated. Be a decent person, talk to us like a friend, and you'll fit right in — the rules are really just for the small number of people who need them spelled out. If a rule isn't listed here, use common sense: would you say this to a friend's living room at 2 AM? If not, don't say it in chat.

// 01 — WELCOME

✓ Welcome.

  • Real questions, real reactions, real personality. Talk to us like a friend would.
  • Multiple languages. English is the chat default, but say hi in any language.
  • Banter, jokes, Pattaya humor. The villa is a room, not a corporate set — same city our restaurant guide covers.
  • Thanking the mods. They work for free — a thank-you costs nothing.
  • New viewers asking obvious questions. Everyone was new once — the format page says the same.
  • Super Chat and Super Thanks. Paid support pins your message and earns a live shoutout — see live-stream support.
  • Watch time without chatting. Lurkers are welcome. Showing up counts.
// 02 — BANNED IMMEDIATELY

✗ Banned.

  • Spam, self-promo links, “follow me” begging. Instant ban.
  • Racism, slurs, harassment, bigotry. Any flavor, any target.
  • Political fights or religious arguments. Not the room for it — same rule as on the FAQ.
  • Explicit / sexual content in chat. We're a public broadcast.
  • Arguing with the mods. They're volunteers. Respect them or leave.
  • Asking for handouts. We're not a charity, this is a show.
  • Hate-baiting or baiting the room into a fight. One inflammatory message can ruin six hours for everyone.
// 03 — ENFORCEMENT

One warning. Then ban.

For most rule-breaking that isn't on the instant-ban list above, mods give one clear warning first. Cross the line again after that warning and you're out — permanently, for that channel. That's the whole policy: short, predictable, no drama. The instant-ban list is in the section above.

We don't negotiate bans in chat, we don't unban because someone apologizes in a DM, and we don't run appeal threads on stream. Mods enforce so Tim and Paemi can host — not so the room can watch a trial. How mod invites work is on the community page. If you got banned, you broke a rule that was already written here.

Instant ban means instant — spam, slurs, sexual content, mod arguments, and self-promo don't get a warning round. Everything else gets one shot to reset.
// 04 — CHAT IS THE ROOM

What chat is for.

On the format page we say it plainly: chat drives the conversation. A question lands and we run with it. A joke takes off and the night becomes that joke. Super Chat pins your message to the top when you want a shoutout or an on-air answer.

That's the deal — you talk, we respond, the room moves. You don't need a script or insider knowledge. Every night stands on its own.

What chat is not for:

  • Music requests. Music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts — chat does not pick songs (locked format).
  • Random calls or outside shoots. The villa is the entire stage for the night (format rules).
  • Debates that split the room. Politics and religion stay out — we keep the room a room.
  • Back-seat producing. We read the room; mods handle flow. You don't need to police other viewers.
// 05 — MODS

Volunteer mods. Not staff.

Mods are invited, never applied for. Be active in chat for 30+ nights, stay respectful, and become known to the regulars — we approach you. Don't DM Tim, Paemi, or anyone else asking to mod. That's an instant disqualifier — full policy on the community mod-invite section.

Mods remove spam, enforce this code, and keep the room readable during a six-to-eight-hour broadcast. They don't speak for the show, they don't share private info, and they don't get named on this website — same rule as on the mods tier. Recognition stays on stream, not on a leaderboard here.

If a mod tells you to stop, stop. If you think a mod made a mistake, finish the night without arguing in chat — we don't relitigate enforcement live.

// 06 — FIRST NIGHT

New here? Start here.

Open the stream at 9 PM Bangkok time (ICT) — or whenever you can after that. The show runs 6 to 8 hours, so there's no such thing as “too late.” timpaemi.com/live always points to the current broadcast. Add the nightly show to your calendar for a recurring 9 PM ICT reminder. Subscribe with the bell on so YouTube pings you when we go live.

Say hi when you arrive. Ask whatever you want. If you want your message read on air, a Super Chat surfaces it — every donation gets a live shoutout. If you just watch quietly, that's real support too: watch time moves the algorithm.

Two minutes on this page and you're set. More questions? See the FAQ or the new-here guide — the rest you'll learn from the room.

One warning. Then ban.

We don't negotiate, we don't unban based on apologies, and we don't read the DMs.

Ready to join the room?

Subscribe with the bell on. Show up tonight at 9 PM ICT. Be cool.
