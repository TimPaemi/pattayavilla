FAQ.

When do you stream?

Every night at 9 PM Bangkok time (ICT). 6 to 8 hours per stream. No nights off.

Where do I watch?

YouTube @timpaemi live, or use timpaemi.com/live as the short link.

Do you take requests?

Chat decides everything in real time. Drop a Super Chat to surface your question or song request.

Can I become a moderator?

Mods are invited, not applied for. Be active in chat for 30+ nights, respectful, and known to the regulars.

How do I support without spending money?

Subscribe with the bell on YouTube, watch live each night, and share the stream link with friends. All free, all matter.

Do you talk politics or religion?

No. Both are banned in chat too.

Will you stream from other places?

Sometimes — Walking Street, Mr K, Lucifer, occasional travel nights. But the Pattaya villa is home base.

Email info@timpaemi.com or message us on LINE (@timpaemi) or WhatsApp (+66 96 728 6999).
