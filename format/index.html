// 01 — A TYPICAL NIGHT

A typical night.

9 PM Bangkok time (ICT, UTC+7). The stream goes live from the villa. We're already there — Tim, Paemi, and a small group of friends. The music is on (copyright-free, set before the stream starts), the cameras are rolling, and chat is showing up. Thailand doesn't use daylight saving — the Pattaya start time never moves. The homepage timezone guide shows when 9 PM ICT lands where you are.

From there it's six to eight hours of challenges, games, food, conversation, and laughter. Paemi cooks. Someone proposes a dare. We argue about something pointless for an hour. Chat throws in a question that derails the entire night. We answer it. We move on. We get loud, we get quiet, we get back to it.

The whole show is us interacting with chat. That's the engine. Chat asks, chat reacts, chat names things — and we play with whatever lands. By 3 AM the room thins, the regulars stay, we wind down, and we sign off.

Every single night since 2024 — weekends, holidays, sick nights, our birthdays. No nights off. Then we do it again the next night.

// 02 — THE CREW

The crew.

A small group of close friends rotates through most nights. It's a family vibe — we hang out, we eat together, we mess with each other on camera. Watch a few nights in chat and you'll learn the room — names stay in the stream, not on this site.

We don't put their names on the website. Only two names ever go here: Tim and Paemi. Everyone else stays in the room, in chat, in the show. That's the deal — same policy as on the community page.

The show is mainly in English. Tim speaks English and German, Paemi speaks Thai and English, and English is the default in chat — but you're welcome to say hi in any language. The show is easy to follow wherever you're from.

// 03 — CHAT IS THE ROOM

Chat is the room.

The show isn't scripted — but it isn't random either. The music is copyright-free, picked and queued before we go live. The villa is where we are, every night, the whole night. We don't go outside. We don't call strangers. We don't chase the next viral moment.

What chat actually drives is the conversation. A question lands and we run with it for forty minutes. A challenge gets thrown and we do it. A joke takes off and the whole night turns into that joke. We watch the room — if it's bored, we change the energy; if it's locked in, we stay where we are.

Chat is the room. We're the hosts, the music is the background, the villa is the stage — and the conversation belongs to everyone who shows up.

This is why we don't take sponsors. A sponsor wants a deliverable. Chat wants a conversation. You can only serve one.

// 04 — THE VIBE

The vibe.

This is not a podcast. There's no episode, no edit, no thesis. It runs, then it ends. The next night is a different show.

This is not a club tour. We're not chasing the next venue and we're not making content out of Pattaya nightlife. We never leave the villa during a stream — no outside footage, no travel nights, no club tours. The villa is the show.

This is not a vlog. We don't dramatize. We don't shoot B-roll. We don't construct a story arc and walk you through it. We are two people in a room with a camera, in a country we love, with the kind of friends who'll sit on a couch with us for eight hours and never ask why.

  • Live only. The room exists in real time — chat is the engine.
  • Villa locked. No outside shoots, no club tours, no travel nights.
  • Unscripted. No episode arc — every night stands on its own.
  • Chat-driven. Questions, jokes, and Super Chats steer the night.

That's the show. Live every night. Pattaya villa. 9 PM ICT. Who hosts it? The about page has the full Tim & Paemi story.

// 05 — YOUR FIRST NIGHT

Your first night.

Never watched before? Here's the short version. Open the stream at 9 PM Bangkok time — or whenever you can after that; it runs 6 to 8 hours, so there's no such thing as late. Add the nightly show to your calendar for a recurring 9 PM ICT reminder.

You don't need to catch up on anything. There's no episode one, no lore, no inside story you missed. Every night stands on its own. Say hi in chat when you arrive — new viewers asking obvious questions are genuinely welcome, and the regulars usually answer before we do.

If you want your message read on air, a Super Chat pins it to the top of the queue and earns a live shoutout. If you just want to watch, that's the whole point too — watch time is the best free support there is. Before you dive in, two minutes on the chat code of conduct tells you exactly how the room works.

That's it. Show up, say hi, stay as long as it's fun. Then come back tomorrow. Still have questions? The FAQ covers the rest.

// 06 — LOCKED RULES

What never changes.

These aren't flexible — they're why the show still works years in. The full list lives on the about page; the chat code covers what happens in the room.

  • We never leave the villa during a stream. No outside footage, no club tours, no travel nights. The villa is the entire stage for the night.
  • No random calls or outside shoots. We don't dial strangers and we don't chase the next viral moment off-camera.
  • No music requests. The music is copyright-free and queued before we go live — chat drives the conversation, not the playlist.
  • No sponsors, ever. A sponsor wants a deliverable; chat wants a conversation. The show is funded by viewers through YouTube — see the support page.
  • We read every Super Chat. Chat questions drive the night; a Super Chat surfaces yours to the top and earns a live shoutout.
// 07 — LIVE VS RECORDINGS

Live is the event.

The live broadcast is the main event. The chat is the room — and the room only exists in real time. That's the whole format: show up while we're live, talk to us, be part of it.

Uploaded recordings stay on the YouTube channel after each stream ends. You can catch up later — but you'll miss the back-and-forth that makes the night. Super Thanks works on those uploads if you want to tip after the fact; Super Chat is for when we're live and you want on-air recognition.
