// HOW TO · SUPER CHAT

Super Chat in 6 steps.

During the live stream only — everything runs inside YouTube. We never see your card and never store payment details.

  1. Open the live stream at 9 PM ICT — @timpaemi live or timpaemi.com/live.
  2. Sign in to YouTube with your Google account. Super Chat requires a signed-in viewer.
  3. Open live chat — on mobile, tap the chat icon under the player; on desktop, use the chat panel beside the stream.
  4. Tap the dollar / Super Chat button in the chat bar (YouTube labels it by region and device).
  5. Pick an amount and write your message. This is what Tim or Paemi read on air — questions, shoutouts, and hellos all welcome.
  6. Pay through Google. Your message pins to the top of chat and earns a live shoutout in the room.

Stuck? Drop a normal chat message — regulars often help new viewers. Read the chat code of conduct before your first night.

// HOW TO · SUPER THANKS

Super Thanks in 5 steps.

After the stream ends — tip any uploaded recording on the YouTube channel. Same Google checkout, no account with us.

  1. Open the channel videos tab — @timpaemi videos lists every upload.
  2. Pick a Pattaya Villa Stream recording from a night you watched (or missed). Uploads stay on the channel after each stream.
  3. Sign in to YouTube with your Google account.
  4. Tap Super Thanks under the video — on mobile, look for the Thanks / heart button below the player; on desktop, it's under the video near Like and Share.
  5. Choose an amount and pay through Google. Your thanks shows on the video and we see it — shoutout energy on the next live night when we spot it.

Want a live shoutout tonight? Use Super Chat during the stream instead — that pins your message in the room.

How support actually works.

Every way to back PATTAYA VILLA STREAM — free or paid — feeds the same engine: YouTube's algorithm. Subscribes, watch time, and shares tell YouTube the villa stream is worth recommending to new viewers. Super Chat and Super Thanks add direct support on top of that.

Every payment runs through YouTube and Google — never through us. Super Chat and Super Thanks are built straight into YouTube's live chat and video pages. You check out with Google, and YouTube passes the support to the channel. We never see your card and never store anything. That single built-in checkout is exactly why there's no PayPal, no Patreon, and nothing off-platform — one trusted platform, nothing to sign up for.

// 04 — SUPER CHAT VS SUPER THANKS

Which one when?

Super Chat pins your message live during the broadcast — use it when you want a shoutout, a question answered on air, or your name read in the room. Tim or Paemi read every Super Chat; it goes to the top of the queue. Step-by-step: Super Chat guide. We don't take music requests — the music is copyright-free and queued before the stream starts — but chat questions drive the conversation, and a Super Chat is the fastest way to surface yours.

Super Thanks supports a specific uploaded recording after the stream ends. The live broadcast is the main event, but recordings stay on the YouTube channel — Super Thanks works on those VODs, shows up on the video, and we see it and say thank you. Step-by-step: Super Thanks guide. Why live beats VOD? See live vs recordings on the format page.

Both run through YouTube. Neither is required to watch — the stream is a public live broadcast with no paywall. Still unsure? The FAQ walks through every option.
